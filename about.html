About Maria Summers

Hello! My name is Maria Summers, and I am originally from Bucharest, Romania. I moved to the United States 11 years ago to attend Brigham Young University in Provo, UT. I obtained my Bachelor's Degree in European Studies with a minor in Communications. I am currently studying for my Master's Degree in TESOL at Brigham Young University. I love learning foreign languages and teaching them to others. I enjoy running, Zumba, biking, reading, and spending time with my husband and two children.
